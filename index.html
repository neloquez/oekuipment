About Us

OEKuipment is an OEK Marine brand.

As OEK Marine grew in naval architecture and engineering, one thing became clear: our clients needed more than design. They needed the right equipment — specified correctly, sourced reliably, and backed by someone who understands the build.

That's why OEKuipment exists.

We are a Rotterdam-based marine equipment distributor, run by a naval architect with over a decade of hands-on shipbuilding experience. The manufacturers we work with bring decades of their own expertise — and together, we don't just sell equipment. We understand it.

We stay involved after the sale. Engineering support, production follow-up, installation coordination — we speak the language of the yard, because we come from it.

New to the Dutch market. Not new to the industry.
