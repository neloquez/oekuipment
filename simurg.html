Embarkation Ladder

SIMURG MARINE’s embarkation ladder is designed for the most demanding maritime environments. Constructed from high-strength manila or synthetic-core ropes and durable hardwood or rubber- topped steps, it fully complies with the stringent requirements of International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea (SOLAS) and the International Maritime Organization (IMO) standards. Designed for safe and reliable access between deck and waterline — in normal operations or emergency evacuation — this ladder is the trusted choice aboard yachts, commercial vessels and offshore platforms.

Pilot Ladder

SIMURG MARINE’s pilot ladder is designed expressly for maritime operations to provide safe boarding and disembarkation of ship pilots — and on occasion other personnel — from a pilot boat to the deck of a vessel. Manufactured to meet the stringent requirements of SOLAS 1974 Chapter V Regulation 23 and ISO 799-1:2019, it features premium hardwood steps (with the lowest four steps reinforced with rubber), high-strength side ropes, and precision-designed spreader steps to prevent ladder twisting. Because marine transfer operations are inherently unpredictable, our certified and meticulously maintained pilot ladder is a critical component in ensuring safe access and protecting lives at sea.
