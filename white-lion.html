Carbon Sun Awnings

Built from lightweight carbon fiber, our sun awning systems combine clean aesthetics with practical, hassle-free use on deck. Each system is custom-designed to match the visual character of your vessel — proportions, color, and finish are all tailored to the project before production begins. Multiple fixing configurations allow the system to adapt to different deck layouts and mounting conditions. Awning poles can also be fitted with integrated accessories such as lighting or speakers on request. Assembly and disassembly are quick and simple, and every set comes with its own transport and storage bag.

  • Custom color, finish, and transparent carbon weave options
  • Lightweight carbon fiber construction
  • Stainless steel deck sockets included
  • Custom awning design to match vessel aesthetics
  • Quick assembly and disassembly
  • Transport and storage bag included

Carbon Boarding Equipment

Our carbon boarding equipment range covers everything needed for safe, clean, and practical deck access — platform protections, deck handles and straps, deck ladders, fender hangers, and boat whips. Every piece is manufactured from 100% carbon fiber, keeping weight to a minimum while ensuring long-term durability on board. All items are produced to order, with custom lengths, thicknesses, and color options available. Stainless steel deck sockets allow quick attachment and removal, making seasonal use and storage easy.

  • 100% carbon fiber construction
  • Custom dimensions — length and thickness to order
  • Color and finish options including transparent carbon weave
  • Stainless steel deck sockets included
  • Quick assembly and disassembly
  • Lightweight, durable, and easy to transport

Carbon Fenders

Our carbon fenders are custom-built for each vessel, shaped and sized to match the platform profile, waterline, and overall hull design. Beyond protection, they double as a functional boarding aid — integrated handles give passengers a secure grip when stepping on and off the yacht. Each fender is manufactured from carbon fiber, keeping weight low without compromising on strength or durability. Stainless steel sockets and lock systems ensure fast, secure mounting to the platform.

  • Transparent carbon weave or custom color finish
  • Lightweight carbon fiber construction
  • Stainless steel deck sockets and lock systems included
  • Custom sizing to match platform dimensions
  • Integrated handles for easy passenger boarding
  • Quick and easy assembly

Carbon Swimming Ladders & Stairs

Our carbon swim ladders and passerelles are designed to give passengers safe, comfortable access to the water — without adding unnecessary weight to the platform. Every unit is built to order, with step count, overall dimensions, and mounting details shaped around the vessel's platform layout and owner requirements. Surface finishes include transparent carbon weave, teak wood overlay, or painted color to match the yacht's design language. Stainless steel sockets and locking systems keep the ladder firmly in place during use, while allowing quick removal and storage between trips.

  • Transparent carbon weave, teak wood, or custom color finish
  • Lightweight carbon fiber construction
  • Stainless steel deck sockets and locking systems included
  • Custom sizing — step count and dimensions to order
  • Quick assembly and disassembly
  • Transport and storage bag included
