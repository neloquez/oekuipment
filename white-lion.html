Carbon Boarding Equipment

Our carbon boarding equipment range covers everything needed for safe, clean, and practical deck access — platform protections, deck handles and straps, deck ladders, fender hangers, and boat whips. All items are produced to order, with custom lengths, thicknesses, and color options available. Stainless steel deck sockets allow quick attachment and removal, making seasonal use and storage easy.
